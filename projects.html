Slide Into High School - APCS 12 ISP

This is a project I created using java in JGrasp for my grade 12 APCS ISP in a group with Charlie To. It is a game that educates middle schoolers about certain aspects of high school, and helps them feel more prepared for this new experience. We created this game to alleviate anxiety related to transitioning into high school. During the creation of this game, I greatly improved my abilities to work as part of a team, in terms of coordination, group planning, and brainstorming together. I also developped my ability to apply coding concepts in java and create graphics. Overall, the game turned out great, and I am happy with what we have achieved.
